THE GOOD DOCTOR

The Good Doctor is an American web series in which it revolves around a Young Autistic Patient name "SHAUN MORPHY". His desire is to become a Surgeon. Due to his inability he got rejected. Unfortunately he got an offer to become a surgical resident at the fictional San Jose St. Bonaventure Hospital under the guidance of Dr.Glassman. Whether he became successful or not? is the main theme of the play.

The series debuted on September 25, 2017. The Good Doctor has received generally mixed reviews from critics, who have praised Highmore's performance but criticized the series' storylines. In April 2023, the series was renewed for a seventh season which is set to premiere on February 20, 2024.
