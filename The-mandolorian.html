THE MANDOLORIAN

The Mandolorian is an American Web series created by Jan Favreau for the streaming of Disney+. The series starts with a lone bounty hunter who goes on the run to protect the Force-sensitive child Grogu from remnant Imperial forces led by Moff Gideon. Whether he succeeded in saving the Grogu from Moff Gideon? is the main theme of this web series.

The series has recieved many positive response from critics, with strong praise to the performance, Visual effects, Cinematography and Direction.
